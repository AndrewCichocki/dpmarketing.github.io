About BuzzBuzzHome

BuzzBuzzHome is North America's most comprehensive and most popular search portal for new construction homes. We connect users directly with developers and provide a social platform for collaboration amongst purchasers, industry experts, and real estate professionals.

How the integration works?

Via this integration you are able to place a Buy Now button on your homes within BuzzBuzzHome. By clicking this Buy Now button, purchasers are guided through a simple and elegant funnel on Daypack whereby they may review and modify their purchase, verify their identiy as per FINTRAC, pay their deposit with a credit card, and digitally sign a purchase and sale agreement. This BuzzBuzzHome / Daypack integration places your sales centre in the cloud.

The largest new home and condo website and community in North America; your complete source of new residential real estate information.
